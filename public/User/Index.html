GraVIT & Go

Darling FC

FC Food, located at the center of the campus, offers a mix of South Indian and North Indian dishes, refreshing fresh juices, and evening snacks. A great spot for a quick and tasty meal!

Darling Cafe

DC Café is a paradise for dessert lovers, offering a tempting selection of cakes, pastries, and their signature doughnuts. Alongside sweet treats, they serve a variety of fast food, including burgers, pasta, and continental delights, making it a perfect hangout spot.

GDN Canteen

A vibrant restaurant serving delicious South Indian and North Indian cuisine, along with a variety of fresh juices and evening snacks like sandwiches. Perfect for a quick bite or a hearty meal!

KC Foods

KC Food is a haven for street food lovers, offering a delightful mix of Punjabi, Gujarati, and Marathi snacks. From spicy Dabeli and crispy Vada Pav to tangy Dahi Puri, every bite is packed with authentic flavors.

Madras Coffee House

Madras Coffee House is the perfect spot for coffee lovers, serving rich, freshly brewed coffee alongside a variety of snacks and refreshing beverages.
